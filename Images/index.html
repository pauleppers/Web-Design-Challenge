Project Summary: Stock Analysis-John Deere

John Deere is the largest Agriculture and Construction Equipment manufacturer in the world. While many factors go into how the company performs, there are some key indicators both short term and long term that can be used to predict the stock price.

After pulling the data from yahoo api, plots were generated for the last years (1y) of data. Factors include Bollinger Bands, RSI, corn prices and soybean prices
