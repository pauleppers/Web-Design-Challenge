John Deere Bollinger Bands Change Over the Last Year

Bollinger Bands are plotted based on standard deviation levels above and below the simple moving average of the price. The period used here is 30 day periods. Bollinger bands help determine wether prices are high or low on a relative basis.

When the bands seperate by an unusual large amount, volatility increases and any existing trend may be ending.The bands are not intended to be used on its own.Based on the divergence of the Bollinger bands, it may be time to sell yet.
